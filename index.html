About Green Skill Hub

Green Skill Hub is a publication and resource platform dedicated to documenting, translating, and amplifying green skills knowledge for learners and professionals worldwide.

Through quarterly publications, curated course updates, and collaboration with initiatives across the globe, we exist to make sustainability education more accessible, actionable, and relevant to real careers and organisations.
